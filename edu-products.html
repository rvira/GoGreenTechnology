EduRobo Products


EduRobo BaseBoards

It is a quickly programmable robot. It is specially designed for beginners in robotics and for all kinds of robotics enthusiasts, starting from School students to Engineering students. Any group age individual can participate in this course. The EDU-ROBO Kit is a very easy to assemble, programmable robotic kit that includes a variety of sensors, motors, controller board, remote control, manual etc. Virtually everything you need to build, code, program and execute the program on Kit. This product is geared towards classroom and group instruction of fundamentals of robotics. And the best part about this course is that you don’t require to come in physically for the learning. You could do it from anywhere in the world, at any time as per your convenience you just need the kit and a laptop.

EDU-ROBO is based on the Atmel microcontroller. The kit is indigenously designed by the PDC of S.P.I.T. It comprises ESP8266 Wi-Fi module, HC-05 Bluetooth module, TSOP1838 IR receiver, HC-SR04 Ultrasonic sensors, proximity sensors and an LCD display. The students will be expected to program each of the on-board components such that there will be complete integration within all the separate modules present on the board. It has two wheels which are motor driven and one castor wheel. There are no prerequisites required to participate in this training program.
