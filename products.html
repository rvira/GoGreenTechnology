Mobile Lab Products


Mobile Analog Board

The mobile analog board is an innovative development board that can be utilized to perform experiments and projects included in digital and analog electronics subjects without the requirement of additional hardware.

The board comes with its own direct and variable power supply along with input and output devices such as IC sockets, a microcontroller, an LCD and LEDs. It also has an onboard function generator used for signal generation. The board also includes a signal conditioning section used for amplifying and attenuating signals higher than 3.3V.

The entire combination of all these parts provides a comprehensive solution that students and teachers can use without using additional hardware. The kit can be easily carried anywhere and is very versatile. It can also be loaded with piggyback boards for additional components.

Baseboard for Analog & Digital

IoT Workbench Board

This board is a sister board to the previous one. It can be used exclusively for IOT based projects and applications. It comes with temperature, light and other sensing devices. It also includes output devices like an LCD, buzzer and LEDs.

The onboard microcontroller can be used for programming the board and interfacing with the various sensors and output parts. It can also be programmed to connect and communicate with a cloud service to maximize the IoT based capabilities of the entire system.

The IoT board makes it easier to implement basic projects that would otherwise require individual power supplies, batteries and separate components

Baseboard for Embedded & IoT

Piggyback Board

Piggyback boards can be used to provide enhanced functionality on top of the existing Analog/Digital and IoT baseboards. The purpose of the board is to enable the user to expand the capabilities of the existing baseboard by providing additional peripherals and elements.

The piggyback boards contain discrete components that might be required for the implementation of experiments or projects specific to a particular subject or domain.

These boards range from the subjects of Linear Integrated Circuits, Electrical Devices, Signal Conditioning and more. They are designed such that they are fully compatible with the baseboard and can be easily attached and detached from it without additional wiring.
